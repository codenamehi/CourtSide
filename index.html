RANK MODE

Rank Mode is the competitive league of Courtside — a structured, season-based ladder where every match counts toward your official rating. We're building it for the mobile app launching soon.

⚔️
Placement matches
Play 10 placement matches at the start of each season to earn your starting rank. No free placements — every game matters.
📈
ELO rating system
Beat a higher-ranked player and earn more. Lose to a lower-ranked player and fall harder. Your rating is a true measure of skill.
🏅
7 rank tiers
Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Diamond → Master → Grandmaster. Each tier unlocks exclusive court privileges at partner gyms.
🗺️
Regional ladders
Compete in your city, your region, or go for the national leaderboard. Rank Mode is geo-aware — climb locally first.
🛡️
Gym territory defense
In Rank Mode, Kings must accept ranked challenges within 24 hours or automatically forfeit their crown. No hiding.
🏆
Season rewards
End-of-season trophies, profile badges, and exclusive gym perks for top finishers. Season 1 launches with the mobile app.
How rank mode works
1
Get placed
Complete 10 placement matches at any partner gym
2
Climb the ladder
Win ranked matches to gain ELO and move up tiers
3
Claim courts
Challenge Kings at gyms — beat them to take the crown
4
End the season
Top players earn rewards, badges, and gym privileges
