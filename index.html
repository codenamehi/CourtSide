The Story

WHAT IS
HOODLYMPICS?

We set up 1v1 matches between real athletes at local gyms — you just show up and play. We handle finding your opponent, booking the venue, and tracking your wins.

🎯

We Find Your Opponent

Tell us your sport, skill level, and availability. We match you with the right player in your area — no scrolling, no awkward DMs.

📍

We Book the Venue

We work with partner gyms and courts near you to arrange the time and place. You just show up ready to play.

👑

We Track Your Wins

Every match result is recorded. Win enough at a gym and you become King or Queen of the Court — until someone takes it.

HOW IT WORKS

1

Sign up

Fill in your details — name, sport, skill level, city, and when you're available. Takes 2 minutes.

2

We find your match

We match you with a nearby opponent at a similar skill level and reach out to confirm the game details.

3

Show up and play

Head to the agreed gym or court at the set time. Play your 1v1 match — we handle everything else.

4

Claim your crown

Results get recorded. Keep winning at a venue and you earn King or Queen of the Court status there.
