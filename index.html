The Story

WHAT IS
HOODLYMPICS?

We're turning every gym, court, and sports venue in your neighborhood into a battleground — giving local athletes a stage to prove they're the best.

📍

Location-Based

Like Pokémon Go but for real athletes. Partner gyms appear on your map as live arenas. The closer you are, the more you compete.

👑

King of the Court

Every gym has a King and Queen per sport. Win enough matches to claim the crown. Lose a challenge and it transfers.

⚡

Instant Challenges

See who's near your gym right now. Send a challenge, pick a sport, confirm the venue — all from your phone.

HOW IT WORKS

1

Create your profile

Sign up, pick your sports, and set your location. Your profile tracks your record, ELO rating, and crown history.

2

Find a gym on the map

Open the live map to see partner gyms near you. Each gym shows the current King or Queen per sport.

3

Challenge a player

Tap any nearby player or the current King to send a challenge. Pick the sport, confirm the gym, and show up.

4

Submit your result

After the match, both players confirm the score. Ratings update instantly. Win the crown and it's yours until someone takes it.
